Privacy Policy

At Magister Consultores, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard data in the course of providing software engineering services.

Data Collection and Use

We only collect and process personal information that is necessary to provide our software engineering services. This may include contact information, project requirements, and technical specifications needed to deliver the requested services.

Any data processed through third-party services or APIs (such as WhatsApp Business API, cloud platforms, or other integrations) is handled solely to deliver the functionality and services explicitly requested by our clients.

Data Protection

We do not sell, transfer, or reuse personal data for any purpose outside of providing the service requested by the client. All data is handled with strict confidentiality and security measures in place.

Client data and project information are stored securely and are only accessible to authorized personnel directly involved in delivering the services.

Third-Party Services

When integrating with third-party services on behalf of clients, we ensure compliance with their respective privacy policies and terms of service. We do not collect or store data beyond what is required for the integration to function as intended.
